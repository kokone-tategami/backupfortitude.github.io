What is optimism bias?
Optimisim bias is a cognitive bias that makes people believe that it is very unlikely for them to be in a negative environment. For this bias, we feature the “Face Reality” skin care products. This product will help with not keeping expectations too high or being too optimistic about their surrounding environment to avoid the shock from the difference between their high expectations and the reality. The products increases the serotonin transporation, meaning that it overall decreases the serotonin levels which contributes to less happiness in a positive way.
